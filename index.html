Welcome to J & J Art Classes

Hey, young creatives! I'm Jude, and I'm all about keeping things chill and colorful. At my art studio, it's not about perfection; it's about the journey of creating something uniquely yours. If you're looking to escape the everyday hustle and bustle and dive into a world of paint, pencils, and endless possibilities, you've come to the right place.

My in-person art classes are a space where you can kick back, relax, and let your imagination run wild. From learning the basics of sketching to experimenting with different painting techniques, we'll explore it all at your own pace. No pressure, just good vibes and plenty of room to explore.

Price: 240 DHS a month, 30 DHS per class

Classes are held every Saturday and Sunday.
